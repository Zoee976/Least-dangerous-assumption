About Least Dangerous Assumption

The Least Dangerous Assumption (LDA) emphasizes presuming competence in individuals with disabilities, fostering opportunities for growth, learning, and inclusion.

Applications in Special Education

Examples of LDA include:

  • Providing access to general education curriculum.
  • Encouraging participation in inclusive classrooms.
  • Supporting assistive technologies for communication and learning.
